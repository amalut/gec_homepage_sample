Overview

The Government Engineering College Sreekrishnapuram (GEC-S) is a technical education institute established in 1999 in Sreekrishnapuram, Ottapalam taluk, Palakkad district, Kerala, India. The college is owned by the Government of Kerala and is governed by the Directorate of Technical Education, Kerala. The college is affiliated to the APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University since its inception in 2015.
